The Eco-Friendly and Sustainable – Electric, is a fully electric car for everyday use, in essence it’s your everyday car. How long does it take to charge the EaS-E?
EaS-E can be charged from a regular household outlet of 220 V 15 A in less than 4 hours depending on the ambient temperature. Charging at a high or low temperature will affect the charging time and charge retention.

What is the lifetime of the battery system?
Basis the cell manufacturers warranty, we anticipate that the cells will offer a lifetime of between 5-8 years depending of the usage of the vehicle. We are using LFP cells which offer a higher life cycle for the cells and are more stable in its operation. We are continuously testing our packs on the field to offer a better data point on the life of the cells.
Does EaS-E support fast charging?
The EaS-E can support charging at 7 Kw chargers, but if done regularly can significantly affect the life of the cells, so while it can charge at a fast rate of 0-80% charge in 60-90 mins, we do not recommend it be used regularly to keep the cells from deteriorating faster than usual.
Does the EaS-E have Regenerative braking ?
The EaS-E has the regenerative braking which allows the vehicle, to capture energy back into the battery, by converting the kinetic energy of the vehicle at higher speeds into charge for the battery pack. As the vehicle slows down the battery pack gets charged momentarily, which adds to the overall range of the vehicle. There are different levels of regeneration that can be selected as per the drivers need. An aggressive regeneration allows for a higher charge on the pack, but will reduce the stopping distance. The hydraulic brake and Regenerative braking can be used together to more rapidly slow the vehicle. This also allows for less wear and tear on the braking system, thus allowing it to run for much longer compared to regular petrol/diesel vehicles.
