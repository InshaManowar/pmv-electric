Our Mission

What you get by achieving your goals is not as important as what you become by achieving your goals.

Climate change & global warming are the biggest challenge of our times. And the way we travel everyday in our cars is one of the biggest contributor to climate change. The ugly signs of climate change are already visible with the increase in extreme weather related incidences just in the last few years. However not all is lost yet, we can still reverse this trend, but we must act now & decisively to reduce the carbon foot print associated how we travel everyday. Going Electric with our everyday personal travel will surely go a long way in our battle against imminent climate change issue.

There is no passion to be found playing small - in settling for a life that is less than the one you are capable of living.

Let’s be honest, solo driving a 3500-4500 Pound Electric car with a range of 300-400 miles per charge, to our office 15-20 miles away every day, isn’t a sustainable way to travel. While we all agree that the future is electric, we must also be practical to the ground realties of our electric grid supply. With the bulk of the electricity still being generated from fossil fuels, the electric car isn’t as green as you think. So to build a truly green electric car, we must right size the electric car, to suit the bulk of the requirement, which is the everyday use. The everyday use of the car is solo, and small distances.

We must build to that, to truly be a sustainable alternative. With the right size of the vehicle itself, & the right amount of range on offer in the car, so that we don’t over build the size of the car itself, and also over build the range of the car, and render an electric car unsustainable.
